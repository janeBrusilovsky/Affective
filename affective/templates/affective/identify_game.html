How To Play

In Game 1, Emotion Recognition, you can practice your ability to identify emotions based of facial expressions. The facial expressions were pulled from Warsaw Set of Emotional Facial Expression Pictures.

Select the emotion that matches the image shown. Each correct answer increases your score, while each incorrect answer loses a life. You are given 3 lives until it's Game Over.
